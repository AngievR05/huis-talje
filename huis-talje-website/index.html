The Story of Huis Talje
Where compassion began with one child and grew into a home filled with hope. Huis Talje's story began in 1999, inspired by one deeply moving event. When a two-year-old boy, severely abused and left with both mental and physical disabilities, had nowhere safe to go, the local community came together in compassion. It became clear that children with such profound needs required a dedicated place of care, love, and specialised support.

At Huis Talje, every detail of daily life is shaped by compassion, dignity, and the belief that every child deserves to thrive. Our approach to care goes far beyond providing a roof and a meal ‚Äî it is about creating a nurturing environment where children with special needs can feel safe, supported, and valued. Each aspect of our care is designed to meet both the emotional and physical needs of every child in our home.

Every action we take at Huis Talje is guided by one purpose: to create a life of dignity, care, and opportunity for children with severe intellectual and physical disabilities. Huis Talje Children's Home exists to provide holistic, compassionate care while building strong partnerships that ensure lasting impact. Our objectives reflect both our daily work and our long-term vision for every child's future.

Huis Talje Children's Home primarily cares for children from previously disadvantaged communities. The heart of our support comes from the surrounding community. Local volunteers form our governing body, contribute their time, and assist with donations of food, clothing, equipment, and labour. Local professionals also provide essential services such as auditing and medical care at reduced rates.

Over the years, Huis Talje has completed many meaningful projects that continue to shape our home today. From renovated spaces to successful community initiatives, these projects reflect the dedication, teamwork, and compassion that keep Huis Talje growing stronger each year.
