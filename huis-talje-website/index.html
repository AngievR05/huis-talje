Our History

The Story of Huis Talje

Where compassion began with one child and grew into a home filled with hope.

Huis Talje's story began in 1999, inspired by one deeply moving event. When a two-year-old boy, severely abused and left with both mental and physical disabilities, had nowhere safe to go, the local community came together in compassion.

On 1 September 1999, Huis Talje opened its doors, founded through the guidance of the Department of Health and Social Development as a home for children with severe intellectual and physical disabilities.

While the government provides a subsidy of R4 500 per child per month, Huis Talje's monthly running costs total R399 915.90. The continued generosity of donors, volunteers, and the wider community makes it possible for Huis Talje to remain a true home of care, warmth, and hope.

How We Care

At Huis Talje, every detail of daily life is shaped by compassion, dignity, and the belief that every child deserves to thrive.

Our approach to care goes far beyond providing a roof and a meal — it is about creating a nurturing environment where children with special needs can feel safe, supported, and valued.

At Huis Talje Children's Home, we provide: Lodging, clothing, and nutritious meals. Safety, security, and stability. Comprehensive medical and dental care. Specialised therapies. Educational and recreational activities.

Every child at Huis Talje is more than a responsibility — they are part of a family built on care, patience, and unwavering love.

Our Mission

Every action we take at Huis Talje is guided by one purpose: to create a life of dignity, care, and opportunity for children with severe intellectual and physical disabilities.

We aim to: Support and strengthen family life. Collaborate with government bodies and organisations. Provide dedicated care for all children referred to us. Offer training programmes. Raise essential funding. Promote awareness nationally and internationally.

Through compassion, collaboration, and commitment, Huis Talje continues to build a foundation of hope and opportunity — one child at a time.

Community Support

Huis Talje Children's Home primarily cares for children from previously disadvantaged communities.

The heart of our support comes from the surrounding community. Local volunteers form our governing body, contribute their time, and assist with donations of food, clothing, equipment, and labour.

While the community continues to give generously, financial support alone cannot meet the centre's needs. We therefore appeal to organisations like yours to help us continue providing essential care.
