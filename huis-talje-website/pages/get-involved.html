Get Involved

Join us in making a difference at Huis Talje. Whether you want to volunteer, donate, or support our projects in other ways, your contribution helps build a safe, supportive community. Feel free how you can lend a hand for the greater good!

Take the initiative to uplift communities through Huis Talje

Volunteer

We welcome permanently or temporarily volunteers at Huis Talje. Whether you want to volunteer for a once, contribute with specific tasks, or join us regularly, we have opportunities waiting for you.

Volunteer With Us

Your time is one of the most valuable things you can offer. When you volunteer at Huis Talje, you become part of a compassionate community that brings light to the lives of others.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Cook & Oversee Care Assistants: Help with feeding, medical activities, mobility support and companionship.
  • Therapy Support Team: Assist with occupational therapy and physiotherapy sessions, encouraging recovery and skill-building.
  • Gardening & Maintenance: Maintain the outdoor areas, beautifying the space for everyone.
  • Events & Fundraising Helpers: Support our fundraising sessions such as drives or music festivals, seasonal parties, and charity dinners (usually once per quarter, no weekly commitment).

Partner With Us

We believe that partnerships amplify impact. When you partner with Huis Talje, you join us on our mission to provide a safe home and life-giving services to vulnerable children.

Types of Partnerships:

  • Program Sponsors: Sponsor a specific program (e.g. music, woodwork/craft or art classes and/or rehabilitation services). Your support can cover equipment, materials, or instructor/therapist fees, ensuring sustainable benefits.
  • In-Kind Donations: Donate goods (food, clothing, school supplies) or services (landscaping, legal) as an in-kind donation.
  • Corporate Sponsors: CSR initiatives that benefit both your organization and Huis Talje. Share our values of hope, compassion and nurture.
